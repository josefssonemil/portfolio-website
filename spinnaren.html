About

As an individual project, we were given a task to design a booking system, for both guests and management, for a small hotel business. A set of requirements of functionality were given, but other than that, we had free hands in how we wanted to implement the required functionality.

My design is exhaustively based on the design guidelines and theory of About Face 4, a book that covers UI/UX design. A thorough rationale can be read in the project report and presentation.
