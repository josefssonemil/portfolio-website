About me

Digital creation has been a part of my life since I was around 11 years old. Since this time (2008), it has exhaustively been a part of both my free time and academic time. To simply put it, I just love it, everything from video editing to UX design.
I hope to continue this journey in the future. With my experience and education I'm aiming for front-end development, but since I have gained experience in many different fields, I have the opportunity to explore and create content in these fields as well.
