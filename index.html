About me

Hey there! I'm a 23 year old student at Chalmers University of Technology, currently in my 5th an final year in the Software Engineering program, where I'm enrolled in the master program Interaction Design & Technologies. I am also currently the chairman of the Master's Reception Comittee IT (MRCIT) that plans and carries out the reception for new students enrolled in the master programmes of the IT division.

Design and programming are two of my major passions in life and have been for over 10 years. Rather than getting bored and looking for other fields to invest my time in, I instead become more and more interested in the fields and seek to learn new technologies, frameworks, trends and guidelines.
